About


What is a prefab


Prefabricated homes, often referred to as prefab homes or simply prefabs, are specialist dwelling types of prefabricated building, which are
manufactured off-site in advance, usually in standard sections that can be easily shipped and assembled


What is a Manufactured Home


Manufactured homes are built in one controlled environment and then transported to the home site.They are fixed to a steel frame so they can remain mobile, and they come with a title instead of a deed like a normal home.


What is a Modular Home


Like a manufactured home or a mobile home, the sections of a modular home are built in a climate controlled home building facility. Modular homes are built to conform to all state, local and/or regional codes that apply based on the final location of the home, just like site-built homes.These homes are on a traditional frame, and come with a deed like a regular house.
